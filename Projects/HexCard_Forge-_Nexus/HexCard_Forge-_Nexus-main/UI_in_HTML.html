Scrollable Lore Panel

In ages past, when stars were young and magic flowed as free as cosmic rivers, the Forgemasters arose. They were beings of immense power and intellect, capable of shaping reality itself through the intricate weaving of aetheric energies and runic technologies...

Their greatest creations were the Nexus Cores, sentient hexagonal constructs that could channel and amplify arcane power, terraforming worlds and birthing wonders. But such power drew envious eyes. The Void Swarm, an interdimensional horror, sought to consume all light and magic.

A great war erupted, shattering galaxies. The Forgemasters, in a final act of desperation, scattered the Nexus Cores across the cosmos, hoping that one day, new champions would rise to relight the fires of creation and stand against the encroaching darkness.

You, Aspirant, are one such champion. The fate of countless worlds rests upon your ability to master the HexCards, rebuild the Forges, and awaken the Nexus.

The path is perilous, fraught with ancient guardians, rival factions, and the ever-present threat of the Void. But within the hexagonal patterns of fate, greatness awaits those bold enough to seize it.
